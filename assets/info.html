Please don't forget that Countdown Days is a widget and it's not an application, so:
  • It can't be moved to the SD card, all widgets should be installed in the internal memory of the device
  • Add the widget to your home screen as follows:
    • Press the Menu button on your device and select Add or tap longer an empty area on the screen with your finger
    • From the Add to Home screen menu please select the Widgets item
    • From the Choose widget menu select the Countdown Days entry. By selecting this entry a new instance of the widget will be created and the Preferences window will be shown.
